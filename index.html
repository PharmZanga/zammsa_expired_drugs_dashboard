Executive Summary

The National Short Expiry and Salvage Dashboard provides real-time visibility of pharmaceutical and medical commodities at risk of expiry across the national health supply chain. The dashboard supports the Ministry of Health, National Supply Chain Coordinating Unit (Control Tower), ZAMMSA, and key stakeholders in monitoring short-dated commodities, coordinating salvage interventions, and tracking progress towards reducing commodity wastage.

The dashboard highlights the total value of commodities at risk, commodities successfully salvaged, remaining exposure, and priority commodities requiring urgent action. It also provides forecasts of upcoming expiries, tracks ongoing salvage initiatives, and monitors implementation of agreed action points by responsible institutions.

Current Reporting Period: This dashboard reflects the latest bi-weekly review and includes commodities identified as being at risk of expiry, ongoing salvage interventions, completed salvage actions, and projected expiries through December 2026.
