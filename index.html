Monitoring rules: Snapshot Remaining Risk = Snapshot Value at Risk - Cumulative Value Salvaged. The permanent salvage register only includes events recorded on or before the selected snapshot. Warning bands are calculated from snapshot date to expiry date: Critical at 30 days or less, High Risk at 31-90 days, and Medium Risk at 91-180 days.
